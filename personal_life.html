My Personal Life

Hi there, I'm Shiv, born and brought up in the city of temples, Varanasi (UP, India). A city very rich in culture, music, art, and education, and the only city that has thrived for the last four thousand years!

As a kid, I was always fascinated by Science and Technology, constantly curious about how things work and the logic behind them. Following my interests, I graduated with a Bachelor of Technology from the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU, Varanasi). During my college years, I was always surrounded by something related to numbers or some analytical process, trends or patterns, whether it was a college project or online games with friends. Therefore, right after my graduation, I embarked on my professional journey as a Data Analyst.

I love going on bike rides, playing online games with friends, solving puzzles, or creating and automating products that enhance my life.
